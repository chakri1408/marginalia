# Working at Nivara

Nivara's culture is built around a simple premise: insurance is often encountered when someone is anxious, short on time, or trying to protect a livelihood. The team therefore treats clarity, accessibility, and operational follow-through as product features.

## The ways we work

### Regional reality is product input

Teams do not treat India as a single market with a language toggle. A flood-prone neighbourhood, a small fleet operator, a first-time health-policy buyer, and a corporate risk manager can all need different evidence, explanations, and service paths. Research, implementation, and support teams feed these differences into product decisions.

### Write the hand-off down

Every client launch has a named product owner, implementation lead, account lead, and technical escalation owner. Work is documented in decision notes and runbooks so customers do not have to rediscover context at each renewal or incident.

### Challenge the convenient metric

Fast quote completion is useful only when it does not conceal unsuitable cover or exclusion confusion. Teams pair speed metrics with exception rates, complaint themes, accessibility feedback, and operational rework.

### Learn in the open, protect the sensitive

Architecture patterns, research insights, and product lessons are shared internally. Customer data, credentials, commercial schedules, and production evidence are restricted to the people who need them. Screenshots and examples are de-identified by default.

## Rituals

- **Monday field signal:** a 20-minute review of implementation observations, service friction, and emerging risk events.
- **Thursday decision clinic:** product, legal, security, and operations colleagues resolve a real ambiguity with a documented owner and due date.
- **Monthly language review:** service copy is checked for plain English, Hindi, and the relevant regional language before high-volume releases.
- **Quarterly recovery drill:** teams rehearse an outage, a delayed partner feed, or a peak-weather claims surge without using production customer data.

## What good collaboration looks like

The strongest Nivara colleagues are precise without being precious. They say when a policy rule is unclear, distinguish a fact from an assumption, and keep a client informed before a small issue grows teeth. They are comfortable moving between spreadsheets, field notes, system logs, and conversations with people who use insurance language differently.

## Inclusion and care

Nivara provides flexible work locations within India, home-office support, mental-health counselling, caregiver flexibility, and annual learning budgets. Hiring panels use structured rubrics and do not treat accent, college prestige, or proximity to headquarters as proxies for ability. The company makes room for regional holidays and encourages teams to plan coverage early rather than making availability a silent burden.
