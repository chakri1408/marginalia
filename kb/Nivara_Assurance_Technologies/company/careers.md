# Careers at Nivara

Nivara hires people who enjoy untangling consequential, messy systems. Our work sits at the meeting point of insurance operations, Indian financial services, accessible software, and applied data practice.

## Teams

| Team | What it owns | Example roles |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Product and design | Product strategy, workflows, content, accessibility | Product manager, service designer |
| Engineering and reliability | Platform services, integrations, security, observability | Backend engineer, SRE |
| Insurance domain | Underwriting, claims, health, life, reinsurance rules | Domain specialist, actuarial analyst |
| Client delivery | Discovery, configuration, training, adoption | Implementation lead, solutions consultant |
| Partnerships and growth | Carrier relationships, broker ecosystems, commercial planning | Account director, partnerships manager |
| Operations | Support quality, finance, people systems, governance | Operations analyst, people partner |

## Hiring process

1. **Context conversation:** we discuss your work, what you want next, and the realities of the role.
2. **Practical working session:** a short, paid exercise or structured case based on fictional, de-identified material—not free client work.
3. **Team conversations:** future collaborators test how you reason, communicate uncertainty, and receive feedback.
4. **Reference and offer stage:** we explain compensation, work location, responsibilities, and any travel expectation in writing.

## What we value

We value durable judgment over performative certainty. A great candidate can explain a complex workflow in plain language, notice who might be excluded by it, and make a sensible next move with incomplete information. Familiarity with insurance is welcome but not mandatory for many roles; we teach the domain rigorously.

## Designations and growth

Every Nivara profile carries both a functional designation and a clear scope of accountability. For example, a Product Manager owns product direction but works alongside an Insurance Domain Lead on rule interpretation, an Implementation Lead on launch reality, and an Engineering Lead on delivery constraints. Seniority is based on judgment, craft, and the ability to improve systems around other people—not simply on managing a larger headcount.

The fictional employee profiles in the [directory](../employees/README.md) describe each person’s career background, current remit, collaboration footprint, and formal designation. They are intended to make the operating model feel like a working company rather than a list of titles.

## Work model

Most roles are hybrid within India, with team anchor days in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Gurugram, Pune, Chennai, or Kochi. Client-delivery positions may require planned travel during discovery and launch periods. We publish travel expectations in each role description and support reasonable accommodation requests.
