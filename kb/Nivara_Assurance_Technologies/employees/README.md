# Nivara employee directory

This directory contains 36 fictional employee profiles. Together they form the staffing model described in [the company overview](../company/overview.md).

## Directory conventions

- Each profile states a primary product interface and, where useful, a client or delivery relationship.
- Product ownership is shared deliberately: a product manager is not the sole decision maker for underwriting, claims, security, or service operations.
- Locations describe team anchors, not a restriction on collaborating across India.

The company has six functional groups: Product & Design (6), Engineering & Data (9), Insurance Domain (6), Client Delivery (8), Partnerships & Growth (4), and Operations & Governance (3).
